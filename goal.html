Goals

Here are goals that we aim to achieve on the local and global level by the year of 2030

Local Goals

Create "Breakfast in Class" programs for schools in Charlotte, there are currently offering this in the Mecklenburg area.

Create local victory gardens across the mecklenburg county

Reduce the amount of kids that are hungry from 1 out of 5 children by 50% (1 out of 10). This will be donating food and contributing to other non-profit organizations in the Charlotte working towards solving hunger (Kids Cafe & Feeding the Carolinas).

Global Goals 1

Help countries that are facing famines and locust plagues.

Provide worldwide education systems on nutrition and food management.

Help grow those whos main insource of income is farming in low GDP/GNI countries by providing financial, technological, and agriculuture assistance.
