Global Impact

Malnutrition affects every country on earth. Cognitive function is severly impacted by malnutrition and impedes people's productivity.

Starvation

Every 10 seconds, a child dies as a result of hunger. The problem is not a lack of food, it is not having consistent access to nutritional food.

Malnutrition
